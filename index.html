IMPORTANT NOTICE

West Ham United can confirm that our Official Club Stores - London
Stadium, Liberty Romford, Lakeside Thurrock and Basildon Eastgate are
now open in line with the latest Government guidance.

SAFETY INFORMATION

  • Only a limited number of people will be allowed in our stores at one time.
  • Face coverings are no longer mandatory, but preferred.
  • Hand sanitiser will be available for customers to use upon entering our stores and will be located throughout.
  • Please keep socially distanced and remain 2 metres apart at all times.
  • A one way flow system will be in operation.
  • Do not enter if you are feeling unwell or have possibly been exposed to Covid-19 within the last 14 days

We thank you for your continued understanding and co-operation.
